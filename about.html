About the Author

Hello, lovely reader! I'm so glad you're here. I'm Morgan Utley, a romance and children's book author who believes that stories have the power to warm hearts, spark imagination, and remind us that love—in all its forms—is the greatest adventure of all.

I’ve always been a natural bookworm. When I was in elementary school, I made frequent trips to the library whenever I finished reading my current Goosebumps book. Not to mention reading the Bailey school kids series, Junie B. Jones and Magic Tree House were rotating favorites of mine. Then, in middle school and high school, I evolved into the world of Harry Potter, Twilight, Hunger Games and Percy Jackson. Romance and Fantasy were my love languages and I often preferred a fictional reality to real life.

No matter what book I picked up, I often thought how cool it was to have someone's name on the cover of a book. Due to that author's original idea and enduring hard work, they were rewarded with something that was entirely their own. I envied it and vowed that someday, I would have a book of my own that I could claim as mine.

And now, it’s been five years since I published my first book and I have no plans of slowing down.
